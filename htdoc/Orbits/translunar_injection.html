In this example I add a Translunar Injection (TLI) burn to an Apollo spacecraft to change its orbit from the initial parking orbit (at 100 nm) to one that puts it on course to intercept the moon's. With out the Lunar Orbit Insertion (LOI) burn, it demonstrates the free return trajectory Apollo 13 used to return to earth. In this case I let the simulation show what might happen if their mid-course correction did not set them on the path to land on safely on earth. With gravitational assists from the moon they would eventually crash on Earth or the Moon or be ejected into deep space like Apollo 12's third stage.
